Why choose Exam Overwatch?

AI based Proctoring System which will be vigilante and provide better performance than Physical proctoring and discourage cheating during On-Site Examination. This project based on computer vision is to assure the principles and integrity of assessments and to prevent cheating. Cheating will be detected using a surveillance camera to identify head movement, hand gestures and body angle movement. It is more accurate and error-free than human labor. This approach is superior to and more successful than the old invigilation system since it does not need as much labor, energy, effort, and time as the previous system.
